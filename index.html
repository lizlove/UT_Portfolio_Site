Digital Archives and Interaction Design

News

23 June 2014 - The University of Texas Austin homepage profiled the digital collection of World War 1 lithographs I produced for the Harry Ransom Center. The cyrillic alphabet presented a particular challenge to the CONTENTdm system, but the depictions of the definitive victory of the Russian porridge over the Austrian sausages were too creative and weird to leave out. To accompany the exhibit, I wrote a blog post on the Ransom Center's Cultural Compass page that discussses lithography and the artists represented in the collection.
