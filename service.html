World War 1 Digital Collection

Harry Ransom Center| Austin, TX

Curated digital collection of lithograph posters from the Texas War Collection in CONTENTdm. Oversaw digitzation and retouched images. Adapted metadata standards and wrote controlled vocabulary for over 150 prints. Drafted finding aid in accordance with DACS standards from existing preliminary inventories. Conferred with American, Russian, French, and German partner institutions to confirm date, title, and translation metadata.

Art Collection Database

Database Management | UT Austin

With a team, built an art collection management database using permanent collection information from the Blanton Museum of Art. The database used PHP and MySQL and has a front-end in HTML/CSS. Database can be searched by the following metadata: artist name, creation date range of the artwork, nationality of the artist, or by selected art term keywords. Produced entity-relationship diagram, relational vocabulary and user stories.
